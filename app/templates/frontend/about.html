Our Story

From Mumbai to the World

Based in the heart of Mumbai, M.A. Tours & Travels was founded with a passion to connect travelers with the most beautiful corners of the world. What started as a dedicated agency curating exceptional journeys to Kashmir has evolved into a full-service travel partner offering curated holiday packages across India and top international destinations.

We believe that travel is not just about visiting new places, but about the seamless and joyful memories you make. From our base in Mumbai, we leverage direct partnerships with premium hotels and transport providers to deliver highly customized, reliable, and premium holiday itineraries tailored to every customer’s dream.
